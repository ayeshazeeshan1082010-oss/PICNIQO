VOLLEYBALL

Beach Volleyball

6-12Players 1-2Hours Competitive

Beach Volleyball ek energetic team sport hai jo sunset beach setting mein aur bhi exciting lagta hai. Is game mein do teams net ke across compete karti hain aur goal hota hai ball ko opponent court mein land karwana. Ye game teamwork, coordination aur stamina ko boost karta hai. Large family gatherings aur group picnics ke liye Beach Volleyball perfect activity hai jo sab ko active aur engaged rakhti hai.

Evening picnic ya family outing ke liye Mini Golf perfect hai kyunki ye friendly competition ke saath fun atmosphere create karta hai. Ye game patience aur focus improve karta hai.

SPIKEBALL

Spikeball

2-4 Players 30-60 Mins Quick Reflexes

Spikeball ek fast-paced beach game hai jo speed aur reflexes ko test karta hai. Is game mein do teams hoti hain aur goal hota hai ball ko net par bounce karna taake dusri team return na kar sake. Ye game picnic aur beach gatherings ke liye perfect hai kyunki ye excitement, teamwork aur healthy competition create karta hai. Short time mein maximum fun ke liye Spikeball best choice hai.
