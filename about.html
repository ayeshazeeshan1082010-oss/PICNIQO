ABOUT PICNIQO

More Than Just Picnics

PicniQo was born from a simple idea: to transform ordinary outdoor gatherings into extraordinary experiences. What started as small family picnics has grown into a community of thousands who believe in the power of togetherness.

We carefully curate every detail - from scenic locations to engaging games - ensuring that every moment spent with us becomes a cherished memory. Our team of passionate event planners works tirelessly to bring joy to families, friends, and colleagues.
