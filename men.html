Men's Games

Discover powerful games and activities designed for men

Beach Water Park Park
BEACH

Beach Cricket

sandy shores · Goa · Puri
6-10 Players Power Hits

Soft sand, harder sixes! 4-over smash between drinks. Popular among men for high jumps, running catches and that tennis ball swing. Teams of 6, no boundaries — just wave to wave.

Team spirit
Competitive
🔥 6-hitting contest 🌊 wet ball fun
WATER PARK

Water Polo

pool · lagoon · water park
5-7 Players High Energy

Ultimate water fight! Teams throw a soft frisbee across the shallow pool. No running, only splash-walking. For tougher dudes – water polo with a floating ball. Great for building team chest bumps.

Team bonding
Refreshing
🤽 water polo 💦 splash tackles
PARK

Park Rough

city parks · community grass
5-8 Players Raw Energy

Traditional men's picnic staple. One man raiding, seven men defending — all in the soft grass. No equipment needed, just raw energy. Often played after potluck lunch. Also variations of kabaddi.

Strength
Strategy
🤚 ankle holds 🔥 do-or-die
BEACH/PARK

Arm Wrestling

beach · park · resort lawn
2 Players Ultimate Test

Ultimate test of pure strength. Men line up, grip hands and push with everything. Often accompanied by loud music and friendly dares. A true test of power and endurance.

Strength
Champion
💪 arm dominance 🏆 champion chair
