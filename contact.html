Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a picnic package?
You can book directly through our website using the contact form above, or give us a call at +91 98765 43210. We'll help you choose the perfect package for your needs.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the event. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a 25% fee. We also offer free rescheduling anytime.
Do you provide all equipment?
Yes! We provide all games equipment, picnic setups, and professional facilitators. Just bring yourselves and your appetite for fun!
Can I customize a package?
Absolutely! We love creating custom experiences. Contact us with your requirements and we'll design the perfect picnic for you.
