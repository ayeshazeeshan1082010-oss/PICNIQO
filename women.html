Women's Games

Discover sophisticated games and activities designed for women

Beach Water Park Garden
BEACH

Beach Frisbee

4-8 Players Teamwork

The most loved women's game at the beach – frisbee. Tossing the disc between friends against gentle waves, running, and laughing non‑stop. It keeps you agile and doubles the fun in the sun.

Builds teamwork
Endless laughter
WATER EDGE

Tug of War

6-12 Players Strength

Tug of war on the beach or in the shallow water – the whole team gets energetic. Digging your feet into wet sand and pulling together, falling and laughing. Women enjoy this game the most.

Team bonding
Perfect for photos
INDOOR

Table Tennis

2-4 Players Fast-paced

Fast feet, quick reflexes, and the satisfying sound of the ball. Perfect for women who enjoy friendly competition. Play in the park pavilion or indoor courts.

Improves reflexes
All ages
WATER PARK

Water Volleyball

4-8 Players Pool/Lagoon

Volleyball with a net inside the water – it's a huge hit at water parks. Tossing the ball in the air, jumping into the water, and cheering for every point.

Swimming + sport
Refreshing fun
WELLNESS

Beach Yoga

Any size Sunrise/Sunset

Start your day with gentle yoga by the ocean. The sound of waves, soft sand beneath your feet, and peaceful stretching. Perfect for women's groups.

Stress relief
Mindfulness
